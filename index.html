Go Find Her at Topsail Island

interview by Katie Park

Today, I had the pleasure of interviewing my roommate, Daniela Ortiz. I interviewd her in our room. I've decided to interview Dani because although we spend a lot of time together, I've never actually had a chance to ask her questions. She grew up in Montgomery, New York. She has an older brother who graduated from St. Mark's last year.

What is your favorite food? Why?

Her favorite food is mac and cheese because her dad makes really good mac and cheese. She liked mac and cheese ever since she was little.

What is your favorite music? Why do you like it?

Her favorite artist is Ed Sheeran but she enjoys listening to all kinds of music. She likes Ed Sheeran becuase most of his songs have good meaning and make her feel good.
